Requirements form:

You are required to have the following;

  • A valid student ID card.
  • A valid student diploma/degree.
  • A valid G.P.A certificate.
  • Must be a student in Zetech,MIT or Harvard university.
  • Must be an ICT student.
  • Must be a final year student doing research or projects.
  • Must be financially stable.
  • Must be a well performing student with a G.P.A of 4.0 and above.
  • Must be a well disciplined.

Please note that without this requirements or in of any contradictions your application will regarded as a failure.
